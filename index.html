ORIGINS

Born in Široki Brijeg, western Herzegovina, forged in the concrete of Zagreb. Ivan Bilinovac operates under the name Noti Limun.

Graphic designer by diploma (Faculty of Graphic Arts, Zagreb, 2022) and rapper by conviction, he is a core member of TDKM, one of the most uncompromising independent hip-hop collectives in the Balkans.

Roots in the gladiatorial arena of freestyle battle rap. Judged by legends Frenkie, Shtela, proving vocal agility and psychological resilience. From the underground cyphers to the Sea Star Festival.
